Proof of Work is special

Proof of Work is fragile

  • Incentives can collapse: See Dogecoin
  • New use cases can change the risk reward model. Tokenization and metaprotocols can increase the frequency of attacks on the ledger
  • Adds value at risk to the blockchain security model
